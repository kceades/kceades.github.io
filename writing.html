Chess

Chess was written as a constraint exercise with the goal of writing a story in exactly 250 words that never used the same word twice. The other option was to not use a certain set of letters: I opted for the non-repeating constraint though because I feel like my writing follows a specific style that wears on itself and its dictionary over a read. It was fun to get creative with sentence structures and vocabulary in general! To read it, click on the pink word 'Chess' above this paragraph.
