Tic-Tac-Toe

Care to try your hand at the classic game? You can play solo vs the computer or against an opponent taking turns clicking on the board. Good luck!

The AI here is a N-ply lookahead algorithm, which currently has a bit of a bug, but still works, just not unbeatable (by any stretch of the imagination).
